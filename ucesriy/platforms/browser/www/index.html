Overview

This application is for creating location based question form.
It should be used with Microsoft Edge Explorer to enable to track your location.

Step 1 - Fill in the question form

1. Fill in the question.
2. Fill in the four answers of the question.
3. Choose the correct answer for the question.

Step 2 - Choose Location

Choose location where you would like question to pop up
There are three methods to do it.
1.Fill in the latitude and longitude manually
2.Click on the map
3.Click "Track My Location" button to track your location.

Step 3 - Upload Question Form

Before uploading the form, please check that all of elements in the form are filled
Upload by clicking publishStart Upload button
If the data upload successfully, there would be messaged "Upload data successfully"
If the data upload failed, there would be error message occurred
